🛍️ **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard**
  - Microsoft Excel | Data Analysis | Business Intelligence

📌 **Project Overview**

The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project developed using Microsoft Excel to analyze online retail sales data and generate meaningful business insights.

This project simulates a real-world business analytics scenario, where raw transactional data is transformed into structured dashboards that help monitor performance, understand customer behavior, and support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project include:

  - 📊 Analyze overall sales performance
  - 🛒 Identify most popular products
  - 👥 Understand customer purchasing behavior
  - 📍 Analyze customer location distribution
  - ⏱️ Evaluate delivery time performance
  - ⭐ Measure customer satisfaction using ratings
  -  📈 Track sales trends over time

    
📊 **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

The dashboard tracks important business KPIs such as:

 - 🛒 Total Orders – Total number of customer orders
 - 📦 Total Quantity Sold – Total products purchased
 - 💰 Total Sales Amount – Total revenue generated
 - ⭐ Average Customer Rating – Overall customer satisfaction
 - ⏱️ Average Delivery Time – Average time taken to deliver orders
 - 📈 Weekly Sales Trends – Sales performance over time
 - 🏆 Top-Selling Products – Most popular products
 - 👥 Customer Gender Distribution – Male vs Female buyers
 - 📍 Customer Location Distribution – Orders by city/location

📂 **Dataset Description**

The dataset contains detailed e-commerce transaction data including:

- Order ID – Unique identifier for each order
- Customer ID – Unique identifier for customers
- Product Name – Name of the purchased product
- Category – Product category
- Quantity – Number of items purchased
- Sales Amount – Revenue generated
- Customer Rating – Customer satisfaction score
- Delivery Time – Time taken to deliver the order
- Customer Gender – Gender information
- Customer Location – Customer city/location

🧰 **Tools & Technologies Used**

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- Data Cleaning Techniques

The dashboard provides a clear visualization of key business metrics such as sales performance, customer activity, product demand, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
